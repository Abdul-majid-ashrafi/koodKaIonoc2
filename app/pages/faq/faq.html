FAQ

Why are ads on Koodka.com free?
We believe that offering free advertising is a convenient, fun, and easy way for people in similar areas to search, buy, and sell their items. It gives you the opportunity to find exactly what you’re looking for or sell to the right buyer in your area. It provides business owners with the exact target audience they need to promote their products or services.
How do I post an ad?
It’s very easy to post an ad on Koodka.com
First you'll need to sign up to site, once you have an account, all you need to do is login to be able to post an ad on koodka.com.
Can I post anything I like?
Koodka.com has rules and policies in place for postings. There are instance where we have to delete ads if we believe that it does not satisfy our rules or policies.
Who can view my ads?
All our visitors can view ads on Koodka.com, even if they are not registered. Remember, the more people see your ads the bigger the chance of your item being bought quicker.
How do I contact someone who has posted a classified ad?
The item details page lists contact information. In most cases there is a telephone number and email address on the listing, where you can contact the seller. However, this site is never involved in any transaction, and does not handle payments, shipping, guarantee transactions and provide escrow services.
How many free classified ads can I post on Koodka.com?
Personal & Business accounts can both post up to 5 free classified ads. If you have other ads currently running, you must either cancel and replace those ads, or wait until they expire. You can choose to run more than 5 ads and for that you can request to activate it when you login to your account.
How long will my ads run for?
Your free ads will run for 1 month. If you would like to continue to run your ads, please renew it once the one-month period is over. If you are business owner and need more time and some special attention, please contact us.
How do I report suspicious ads?
We work very hard in keeping our site clean. However due to huge number of posting there can be times when someone edits his/her post when it’s already verified. Once you notice something suspicious please press the ‘report’ button on the specific ad to inform us, or contact us on report@koodka.com
